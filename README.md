# HoloScape
 
 Unity Version: 2018.4.28f1
 
 Story:
 
 You are Holo Han a hologram stuck in his holographic world. 
 
 Trying to escape is dangerous!! You will encounter many obstacles!! Such as enemies, puzzles,bosses!
 
 
 
**Controlos**

 W A S D to move player.
 
 E to use.
 
 F9 to show FPS
 
 ESC to pause.
